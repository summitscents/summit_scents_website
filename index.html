Our Story

Born at the Summit

Summit Scents was founded on a single belief: that fragrance is the most intimate expression of identity. We set out to create something that felt as elevated as the mountains that inspired us — clean, powerful, and enduring.

Every bottle of Ivory is crafted in small batches using ethically sourced ingredients. We work directly with perfumers in Grasse, France to ensure every drop meets our obsessive standard for quality.

We don't make fragrances for trends. We make them for legacies.
